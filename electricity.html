Electricity Usage

Simple and effective ways to reduce your electricity consumption

Switch to LED Lighting

LED bulbs use up to 75% less energy than traditional incandescent bulbs and last 25 times longer. This simple switch can significantly reduce your electricity consumption and lower your energy bills while providing the same quality of light.

Use Smart Power Strips

Combat phantom energy loss with smart power strips that automatically cut power to devices in standby mode. These intelligent strips can detect when devices aren't in use and completely shut off power, preventing unnecessary electricity consumption.

Unplug devices when not using

Unplugging devices when not in use can avoid phantom energy drain and cut down electricity waste. Also reduce your electricity bill.

Make the most of natural light

LED bulbs use up to 75% less energy and last 25 times longer than incandescent lighting. Switch to LEDs to save energy and reduce costs.
