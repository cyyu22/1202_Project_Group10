Water Usage

Smart and simple ways to conserve water at home

Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth

Turning off the tap while brushing your teeth can save up to 200 gallons of water per month. That's a lot of water!

Fix Leaks Promptly

A dripping faucet or leaking toilet can waste thousands of liters of water per year. Regular maintenance prevents hidden waste and reduces bills.

Use shower instead of bathtube

Showering uses less water than taking a bath. You can save up to 50 gallons of water per bath by taking a shower instead!

Use the dishwasher

Using the dishwasher can save up to 100 gallons of water per week compared to washing dishes by hand. Save time and water!
